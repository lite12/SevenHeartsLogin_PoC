It's Halloween at SevenHearts!

October 9, 2007


Some of you probably saw this coming if you`ve been following the forum drama & discussion lately, Gaia is the PK server that was promised here. Although this is not the November announcement or our next update, it`s more like something players can enjoy before then and between larger patches. I want to stress that this is running on a trial basis to see if it is as good for the community as we would hope it is, the PK server will be behind on significant updates and specifically the November update won`t be applied to PvP server right away, if at all. More on that later.

We encourage everyone to try out the new server and give us feed back. Stockpile still works. P2P isn't possible right now as this is a trial basis, if feedback is positive we`ll add some means of P2P on the PvP server as well.

I know some players will be worried about Seven Hearts jumping on the HR bandwagon and I want to clear that misconception immediately. We`ll be doubling our efforts on the main server to make sure this is the right place for both high rates and iROSE nostalgics.

Seven Hearts v767-768 Patch

July 30, 2007

 Seven Hearts v767-768 Patch Notes:

  • NPC Equipment.
    • Shops in Eucar now carry a range of weapons appropriate for levels 80-9x.
  • New NPC/MOB Equipment.
    • An assortment of new backitems, masks and weapons have been added as drops to various NPCs on all 3 planets, with the requirements appropriate for the level of the NPC they drop from. Black and red astarot wings have also been added to Astarot Kings, they drop at rate slightly lower then the blue astarot wings. More information here.


  • New Ability Parts.
    • Moldie Sunroof & Moldie Sunroof Pink (both PAT Ability parts) are sold by Sergei in the city of Eucar.
  • Job Specific Equipment.
    • Class specific equipment for Lev 170, 185, 200 and 210 are included in this update with stats that have been refitted to follow the iROSE style hierarchy. See the patch preview for more images.
  • New Crafts and Drops.
    • The bulk of this update comes from the meticulously planned crafts and drop tables for Eldeon. Level 170 and 185 Equipment can be crafted by Artisans with the appropriate skills at max level. Armours for level 200 and 210 are drop-only, more information in the following points.
  • High Level Weapons.
    • Evo weapons have been implemented and refitted for level 170 and 185. Our plan is to provide alternatives and higher level weapons with 7h exclusive content, coming in the near future.
  • Exclusive Materials.
    • All materials have been extended with higher quality parts to match the lower success rates and considerably more challenging crafting requirements of 170-185 equipment. Both common and rare monster-specific materials drop from each monster type on Eldeon to ensure well balanced crafting requirements.

  • Zombie and Underworld Chests.
    • Upper level armour for levels 200 and 210 work somewhat differently from any of the categories coming before it. Rather then hunting for specific weapons or crafting materials from certain NPCs, Lev 200 & 210 equipment come from Zombie and Underworld Chests which drop with a very low chance in Sikuku Underground Prison. Each chest contains a random piece of equipment with base stats, the type of item is only determined when the chest is opened. Please note that we will be following up in a coming update with an NPC that augments stats of high level equipment.

  • Pet Shops.
    • Lampa in Zant, and Siricu in Junon Polis sell pet capsules for low and mid-range levels.
  • SevenHearts Exclusives.
    • Continuing the trend of new Seven Hearts unique weapons- Obsidian Katar drops from two high level NPCs on Eldeon. Glass wings, a Seven Hearts exclusive back item, has been added to the crafting list.

  • New Music Tracks.
    • 11 New BGM tracks were included in this patch, the following maps were affected:
      • Anima Lake
      • Breezy Hills
      • Faction War
      • Forest of Wisdom
      • Junon Clan Field
      • Lions Plains
      • Luxem
      • Marsh of Ghosts
      • Planetary Warp
      • Prologue ( opening sequence )
      • Sikuku Underground Prison
  • Cosmetic Adjustments.
    • Minor adjustments were made to the Scarab Hander`s appearance.
  • Bug Fixes.
    • The missing sounds and projectiles are patched for: Gargoyles, Icanes Leader, Icanes Engineer, Sikuku Infultrator.
    • An integer overflow in EXP for characters > Lev 204.
    • An issue where Turaks would respawn continuously, allowing anyone to gain unreasonably large amounts of EXP by stacking the damage against several respawned Turaks.
    • Faces 4 and 5 from the designer NPC selected the same face.
    • P.I.G. and Grunter cart consuming fuel.
    • Exp in Sikuku Underground Prison was lowered significantly.

Seven Hearts v765-v766 Patch

July 10, 2007

 765 Updates List:

  • Mt. Eruca Drop Tables.
    • Hearts and refines drop less on Mt. Eruca then they used to, zully is the prefered drop for all monsters.
  • New Mask.
    • Il Cigar Classico. A fine cigar for fine gents. Nicely wrapped in natural tobacco leaves, its taste focuses ones busy mind, despite disadvantages. - A Seven Hearts original item, by fugiisland and Camia.
  • New Carts.
    • The P.I.G. Cart and P.I.G. Grunter Frame are sold in each faction shop. The Grunter Frame is also a Seven Hearts original, designed by BlackWidow.
  • Hair Stylist NPC.
    • Players can now chose between an assortment of colors and hairstyles ( some unique to the server ) through a hair stylist NPC in Junon Fields. Gender and face changes are also offered - for a price.
  • AM Union Wars.
    • The morning union wars are initiated automatically from now on rather than manually activated by GameMasters.
  • Cosmetic Changes.
    • One of our goals at Seven Hearts is to make day-to-day tasks more enjoyable and unique. We will occasionally make cosmetic changes to the GUI or parts of the game we feel are underwhelming. Today`s patch changes the appearance of zully boxes, possibly the most common drop item- and equips Hunter summons with bows which scale according to the level of the summon. The final Hunters are equipped with the Six Stick Bow.
  • Bugs Fixed.
    • Several fixes: disconnecting when using the warp gate between Forest of Wandering and Shady Jungle, Client crashes from certain Eldeon monsters ( mainly Gargoyles ), Revised stats for the Icanes and a fix for certain items becoming invisible when dropped, including the Scarabhander.
  • Party Bridging Formula.
    • Bridging with players more than 40 levels away from the average level of your party is viewed by our team as both an exploit and generally poor game design. To combat this the server applies a formula for new members entering the party, which, allows roughly a difference of 40 levels from the party`s average level. More information is available in the forums.
